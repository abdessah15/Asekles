Anemhal anaẓuṛan n usisleg, asegbar

IMUḤUCEN

Tawsit : Taḍṣa, Igerdan

Azemz n usufeɣ : 2025

Aglam n usaru :
Reda, Ɛisa, Ɛliluc, Ḥmimuc d imuḥucen, d iɛlaḍ. Yal tikkelt ara binen sewjaden taxnanast iwakken ad rewlen seg lḥebs. Deg lḥebs yella ccambiṭ Muḥend Waɛli akked Haniya, war asṭuqqet n wawal, atan ihi tezrart-a ar ɣur-nwen.
