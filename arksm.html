AREKSIM

Tawsit : Inig, Amuggan, Arrac

Azemz n usufeɣ : 2026

Aglam n usaru :
Asaru-a yettmeslay-d ɣef Areksim. D aqcic i d-tesker terkeft n wuccanen deg teẓgi. Seg mi yella d agrud, d BUBBAZ ursel akked MAYAS Amayas i t-yesselmaden iṣudaf n teẓgi. AREKSIM ḥemmlen-t yakk yiɣersiwen siwa aɣilas amcum BUJƐIQ i yebɣan ad yeskef idammen-is wa ad ikedded iɣessan-is, almi d ass i yeḍra wayen yeḍran ifaq-d Areksim belli yekka-d seg yimdanen, ur yelli d aɣersiw.
