Berhuc d Salas

Tawsit : Inig, Amuggan, Arrac

Azemz n usufeɣ : 2026

Aglam n usaru : Deg tlemmast n ṭṭrad amaḍlan wis sin, deg yidurar n Fransa, imezdaɣ n yiwet n taddart tamecṭuḥt, ideg d-tlul tayri gar uydi d umdan amecṭuḥ, aydi-nni i ɛezlen wat taddart imi ɛudden d netta i ittetten lmal-nni i ttaǧǧan deg uddaynin, maca yiwen n wass tbeddel tmuɣli, ufan-t d netta i yellan yesseḥbibir fell-asen seg wuccanen.
